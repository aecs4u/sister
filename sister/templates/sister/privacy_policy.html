Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

AECS4U Srl respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use SISTER.

2. Data We Collect

When using SISTER, we process:

  • Authentication data: Email address and identity information via Clerk SSO.
  • Query data: Cadastral search parameters you submit (province, municipality, sheet, parcel, tax codes).
  • Results data: Property and owner information retrieved from the SISTER portal.
  • Usage data: Request timestamps, query types, and service health metrics.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To process your cadastral search requests.
  • To store search results for your future reference.
  • To improve service reliability and performance.

4. Data Retention

Search results are retained in the database for the configured TTL period (default: 6 hours in cache, permanent in database). You may request deletion of your data at any time.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (TLS), secure authentication (Clerk SSO), and access controls to protect your data.

6. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: privacy@aecs4u.com
